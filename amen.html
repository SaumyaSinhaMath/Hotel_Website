AMENITIES

     DINING:

  • Enjoy the food prepared for you by our traditional cooks and supervised by the lady of the house while absorbing the traditional ambience of our cosy and private dining room. Khwabeeda Palace has a delectable range of Indian cuisine to offer.
  • There is 200 seating capacity, 50 at pool side, 50 in the garden area and remaining inside the palace.

     PARTY HALL:

  • Whether you are planning a lavish wedding or a small intimate affair, we provide the best-in-class service to make your occasions memorable.
  • Our grand ballrooms, rooftop venues with 360 degree city views & state of the art banquet halls will dazzle your guests!
  • Booking on arrival

     SWIMMING POOL:

  • Enjoy a rejuvenating swim in the azure depths of our swimming pool. Or you could just choose to sit by the pool and read a book.
  • While you enjoy yourselves at the poolside, your kids can explore the Children's Swimming pool.
  • It is highly maintained and the customers have to follow strict hygiene rules to ensure safety.

     KAYA KALP- THE SPA:

  • A fragrant haven that melts away the stresses and strains of daily life, with both ancient and modern treatments practised by trained therapists. This award-winning spa is spread across two floors.
  • Booking on arrival

     SOUVENIR SHOP:

  • We have a beautiful collection of Rajasthani and Indian souvenirs for you to have a remembrance of your holiday!
  • Decently priced gifts and souvenirs can be bought from our in-house shop.

     TRAVEL DESK:

  • Our travel desk offers car rental, Rajasthan tour packages and Jodhpur sightseeing trips. You can also enquire and book bus and train tickets for your onward journey.
