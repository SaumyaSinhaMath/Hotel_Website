A flamboyant showcase of Rajasthani architecture and flair at its most irresistible, JODHPUR has long been established on tourist itineraries. As with most of India, Jodhpur too, offers a unique combination of the ancient and the modern architecture. While you can relax in the evenings in modern pubs, you can discover the massive ancient forts during the day.

Immerse yourself in the architectural splendor of the Khwabeeda Palace in Jodhpur. Stroll around the 15 acres of well-kept lush gardens in the palace complex and spot peacocks all around. This palace complex also has 240 rooms, a private meeting hall, an exquisite throne chamber, a Durbar Hall, a vaulted banquet hall, a ballroom, private dining halls, an indoor swimming pool, spa, a library, a billiards room, two unique marble squash courts, four tennis courts, and long passageways.

If you plan on visiting Jodhpur and are wondering how to reach Khwabeeda Palace, you can travel by the Rajasthan State Roadways buses from the different cities of Rajasthan to Jodhpur. From here, you can take a cab or taxi to the Khwabeeda Palace. If you are driving till here in your own vehicle, take the Airport Road and then travel by the 3rd exit from the roundabout to reach the SH 61. Next, turn left and then go through another roundabout to reach the Khwabeeda Palace.
