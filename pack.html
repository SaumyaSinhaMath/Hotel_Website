Jodhpur- The Blue City

It is a city in the Thar Desert of the northwest Indian state of Rajasthan. Its 15th-century Mehrangarh Fort is a former palace that’s now a museum, displaying
weapons, paintings and elaborate royal palanquins (sedan chairs). Set on on a rocky outcrop, the fort overlooks the walled city, where many buildings are painted
the city’s iconic shade of blue. Travellers come here to visit astonishing destinations in and around Jodhpur.
